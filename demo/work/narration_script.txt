You can write down how you feel every day for a month and still have no idea what is actually moving your mood. SoulSync is built to answer that. Logging a day takes about ten seconds. Pick how you felt, then tap what shaped it. Your sleep, the people you saw, the things you did. The more honest you are, the more it can show you later. Here is where it earns its place. SoulSync reads your own entries and tells you the truth in plain words. The days you feel your best, the days you struggle, and the one thing that lifts you the most. And every word of it is worked out right here on your phone. Nothing is uploaded, nothing is sold, no account needed. Free, and open source.
